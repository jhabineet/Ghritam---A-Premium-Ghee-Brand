# **App Name**: Ghritam

## Core Features:

- Homepage Sections Display: Render the complete homepage, including hero, split section, highlight, product catalog preview, and 'Why Choose Ghritam' sections as described.
- About Us Page Display: Render the dedicated 'About Us' page featuring a brief company description and a team members section with square photos.
- Contact Page Display: Render the 'Contact' page, displaying essential contact information, a clear message, and the highlighted phone number.
- Responsive Navigation System: Implement a sticky navigation bar across all pages with 'Home', 'About Us', 'Contact' links, and a prominent 'Contact for Booking' button, alongside a responsive hamburger menu for mobile.
- AI Hero Image Generator Tool: Utilize AI to generate a realistic hero image based on the specific prompt, depicting a rural Indian village with a bowl of ghee, embodying natural and premium aesthetics.
- AI Highlight Text Generator Tool: Leverage AI to craft unique, italicized, and quoted descriptive text for the 'Highlight Section' that captures a tone of purity and traditional craftsmanship.
- 'Why Choose Ghritam' Accordion: Develop an interactive accordion or expandable dropdown component for the 'Why Choose Ghritam' section, allowing users to expand points of interest.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary interactive color: A rich, golden yellow (#D4A017) to symbolize the premium quality of ghee, used for buttons and key call-to-actions. (HSL: H=44, S=73%, L=46%)
- Background colors: A soft, warm off-white ghee tone (#FFFDF7) will be the dominant background color for most sections. Pure white (#FFFFFF) will also be used for specific contrasting sections. (HSL of ghee tone: H=48, S=10%, L=99%)
- Secondary accent color: A deep, earthy reddish-brown (#551A0A) for subtle accents and elements that evoke tradition and heritage. (HSL: H=14, S=60%, L=25%)
- Text color: Dark gray (#333333) will be used for all body text to ensure high readability and maintain the clean, premium aesthetic.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) will be used for titles and prominent headings, conveying an elegant, traditional, and high-end feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) will be used for body text, ensuring clear readability and a modern yet warm tone that complements the elegant headlines.
- Implement a consistent set of clean, line-based or subtly filled icons throughout the user interface, supporting the minimal, premium, and traditional aesthetic.
- The layout will feature a fully responsive, mobile-first approach with meticulous attention to clean spacing, alignment, and modular design for an organized user experience.
- Integrate smooth scrolling, subtle fade-in effects, hover animations on interactive elements (buttons, cards), and smooth transitions between sections for a polished and engaging UI.
